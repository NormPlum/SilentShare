<p>If you're standing at a store checkout or reception desk and need to tell
someone your personal information (such as your phone number or date-of-birth),
anyone standing nearby can overhear it. Instead, you can use Silent Share to
visually display the information on your phone in a large, easy-to-read
font.</p>

<p>The main screen shows the default user account ('Me') and some dummy fields
('Name' and 'Phone'). Use the floating Edit button to add, edit and remove
fields. Fields can have any name and value you like, and you can add as many
fields as you like.</p>

<p>To show someone your information, from the main screen check the boxes next
to the fields you want to show, then press the floating View button.</p>

<p>You can add other users' information too (or just divide your own information
between multiple <em>accounts</em>). Press the User icon on the main screen to
see a list of users. Press on a user to see and edit their information, or use
the floating button and menu to add or edit/delete users respectively.</p>
